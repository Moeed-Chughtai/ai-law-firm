SAFE (Simple Agreement for Future Equity)

This SAFE is entered into as of January 15, 2024 by and between TechCo Inc., a Delaware corporation (the "Company"), and Angel Investor LLC (the "Investor").

1. Investment Amount

The Investor hereby invests Five Hundred Thousand Dollars ($500,000) in the Company.

2. Valuation Cap

The Valuation Cap for this SAFE shall be Eight Million Dollars ($8,000,000). This means that upon conversion, the Investor's shares will be priced based on the lower of the Valuation Cap price or the price per share in the Equity Financing.

3. Discount Rate

The Discount Rate shall be twenty-five percent (25%). Upon conversion, the Investor shall receive shares at a price equal to the lesser of the Valuation Cap price or the Discount Price (which is the price per share in the Equity Financing multiplied by 0.75).

4. Conversion Events

This SAFE will convert into shares of Safe Preferred Stock upon the occurrence of:
(a) An Equity Financing in which the Company issues and sells Preferred Stock at an aggregate purchase price of not less than $500,000;
(b) A Liquidity Event; or
(c) A Dissolution Event.

5. Pro-Rata Rights

The Investor shall have the right, but not the obligation, to participate in subsequent financing rounds on a pro-rata basis. This right shall be available to all Investors regardless of investment amount.

7. Representations and Warranties

The Company represents and warrants that:
(a) It is duly organized and validly existing under the laws of the State of Delaware;
(b) It has full power and authority to execute this SAFE;
(c) The execution of this SAFE does not violate any existing agreements.

8. Governing Law

This SAFE shall be governed by the laws of the State of Delaware. Any disputes arising under this agreement shall be resolved through binding arbitration in San Francisco, California.
