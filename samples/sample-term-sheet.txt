TERM SHEET
Series A Preferred Stock Financing

TechCo Inc. (the "Company")
January 15, 2024

INVESTMENT TERMS

1. Offering Terms

The Company will sell Series A Preferred Stock in a financing round of $5,000,000 at a pre-money valuation of $20,000,000. The price per share shall be determined based on the pre-money valuation divided by the fully-diluted capitalization.

2. Liquidation Preference

The Series A Preferred Stock shall have a liquidation preference of 1x participating preferred. In the event of any liquidation, dissolution, or winding up of the Company, holders of Series A Preferred Stock shall receive:
(a) First, an amount equal to the Original Purchase Price plus any declared but unpaid dividends; and
(b) Second, their pro-rata share of the remaining assets on an as-converted basis.

3. Board Composition

The Board of Directors shall consist of five (5) members:
- Two (2) members designated by the holders of Series A Preferred Stock
- Two (2) members designated by the holders of Common Stock
- One (1) independent member, who must be approved by the holders of Series A Preferred Stock

4. Protective Provisions

The consent of holders of a majority of the outstanding Series A Preferred Stock shall be required for:
(a) Any sale, lease, or transfer of all or substantially all of the Company's assets;
(b) Any merger, consolidation, or reorganization;
(c) Any amendment to the Company's Certificate of Incorporation or Bylaws;
(d) Any issuance of new securities (except for employee stock options);
(e) Approval of key personnel hiring decisions, including C-level executives;
(f) Entry into contracts or commitments exceeding $50,000 in value;
(g) Any declaration or payment of dividends.

5. Anti-Dilution Protection

The Series A Preferred Stock shall have full ratchet anti-dilution protection. If the Company issues additional shares of Common Stock or securities convertible into Common Stock at a price per share less than the Conversion Price, the Conversion Price shall be adjusted downward to such lower price.

6. Drag-Along Rights

If holders of more than 50% of the outstanding shares (voting on an as-converted basis) approve a sale of the Company, all other shareholders shall be required to participate in such sale on the same terms and conditions.

7. Information Rights

Investors holding at least 100,000 shares of Series A Preferred Stock shall be entitled to:
(a) Annual audited financial statements;
(b) Quarterly unaudited financial statements;
(c) Annual budget and business plan.

8. General Provisions

This term sheet is non-binding except for the provisions regarding confidentiality, exclusivity, and expenses. The parties agree to negotiate in good faith to execute definitive documents within 30 days.
